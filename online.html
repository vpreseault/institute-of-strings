Lesson 3: Basic Techniques

Take your violin playing to the next level by mastering fundamental techniques. In this lesson, you'll learn proper finger placement, essential shifting techniques for navigating the fingerboard, and the expressive art of vibrato. These techniques will lay the groundwork for your journey to becoming a proficient violinist.
