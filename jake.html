Jake Mitchell

Jake Mitchell is a versatile violinist with a passion for both performance and education. He earned his Bachelor's degree in Violin Performance from the Curtis Institute of Music and has played with several prestigious orchestras, including the New York Philharmonic and the Boston Symphony Orchestra.

Jake's teaching method emphasizes musical expression and technical excellence, encouraging students to explore a wide range of repertoire. His engaging and supportive teaching style helps students build confidence and achieve their musical goals. Jake has a proven track record of helping students excel in competitions and college auditions.

Jake also offers online lessons for students who prefer to learn at their own pace. These lessons are meant for beginners to kickstart their violin journey before booking an in person lesson.
