Emily Roberts

Emily Roberts is an accomplished cellist with over 15 years of teaching experience. She holds a Master's degree in Music Performance from the Juilliard School, where she studied under some of the most renowned cellists in the world.

Emily's teaching philosophy centers on nurturing each student's unique musical voice while developing a strong technical foundation. She has successfully guided students of all ages and skill levels, from beginners to advanced players preparing for conservatory auditions. Emily's friendly and patient approach makes her a favorite among her students.
