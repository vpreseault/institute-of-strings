Lisa Chen

Lisa Chen is an experienced violinist and violist, as well as a dedicated instructor, with a Master's degree in Music Education from the University of Southern California. She has performed internationally as a soloist and chamber musician, bringing a wealth of performance knowledge to her teaching.

Lisa specializes in working with young students and beginners, making her an ideal choice for parents seeking a nurturing and inspiring instructor for their children. Her lessons are designed to be fun and interactive, fostering a love for music while building strong technical skills. Lisa's students consistently achieve high marks in exams and enjoy participating in recitals and local music festivals.
